PAYMENT INSTRUCTIONS

Kid GLAMMMM uses Litecoin (LTC) for payments.

  1. You email me and let me know what you want to order
  2. I email you back with a payment invoice telling you exactly how much to pay in Litecoin (LTC) and I give you a special LTC address to make your payment to.
  3. You can scan the QR Code I email you with your crypto app on your phone such as Trust Wallet and make the payment
  4. I will monitor your payment address to see if a payment has been made and once it receives enough confirmations on the blockchain, I'll process your order.
  5. At some point in the process I will need your shipping address and name. You can provide those to me after you make your payment. If you'd like to email me once you make the payment and include a screenshot showing the transaction number that would be great.
  6. I'll try and keep you informed about when your item ships and any tracking number that I can get if one is available. So you can track your own package.
  7. And of course, you can always email me if you have any questions about your order. Or problems that need my attention. I want to make sure you get what you paid for. Even though I call it a donation.
  8. But you do realize that not everything is 100% within my control. Like I don't make the T-shirts myself. Someone else prints them and drop ships the order. So if they screw up and won't help me with any type of refund then I didn't get any money back either. And we might be able to try one more time.
